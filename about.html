About Me

I am Graphic Designer based in Amaranth

Hey there! I'm Nathanael Delivuk a graphic design student currently expressing my creativity by turning ideas into visually stunning realities, at Humber college. To be completely honest I never saw my self becoming a graphic designer, but ever since I discovered I can use my skills and hobbies such as photography, motion design and mixed media creations to create eye catching designs, I was hooked.

My Journey as a student has been a mix of learning and growth from learning to work collaboratively with others to reach a shared goal to developing a keen eye for detail and a toolbox of design software skills. However my journey as a student is never finished as I am always down to learn something new. Outside the realm of pixels and vectors, you can find me exploring the wilderness that is Canada seeking inspiration in the beauty of nature, or with a wrench in hand working on my project car while brainstorming my next creative endeavour. Let's connect and explorethe endless possibilities of design together! I am always down to learn something new.
